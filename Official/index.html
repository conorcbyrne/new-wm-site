EP Release

Their highly anticipated EP includes their first single Pen to Paper, which was released back in March 2020. Originally scheduled to release in July of 2020, the EP reached an unfortunate pause in its production due to the pandemic. But finally, through hard-work from the musicians and the producers, the record is finally coming.

Stories We’ll Never Tell was a loose concept project that focused on the songwriters’ deepest stories that can often be the most difficult to express. Using the power of music, each song will tell a different tale, or a mixture of a couple, of intimate and complex scope. The band wear their hearts on their sleeves in this EP, bringing something to the table that almost everyone can relate to, even on a subconscious level.

Music Video Release

Not only have Warped Minds got their EP arriving, but the band have also announced the release of their brand new music video for Pen to Paper, premiering on deMars Live, and available to view afterwards on their YouTube channel on February 20th.

Who We Are

Warped Minds are a four-piece alternative rock band from Dublin. While all members of the band knew each other and played in musical projects together for years before, the band officially formed in 2014. Through various projects and hiatus’, the band finally found their stride in the summer of 2019, when their songwriting and on-stage presence took a much more cerebral and entertaining turn.

Their truly unique aspect is their rich vocal harmonies and swapping lead vocals. Each song of theirs has a different lead singer, split between frontman & guitarist Paul Downey, guitarist Conor Birkett and bassist and pianist Ciarán Berry, with support vocals coming from drummer Conor Byrne.
